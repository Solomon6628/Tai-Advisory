About TAI Advisory

An AI Policy Consultancy with a Global Justice Lens

Founded by Harvard Law graduate and former Meta policy lead Chukwuebuka Ogbodo, TAI Advisory bridges the gap between technology, law, and human rights. We specialize in advising on AI governance and digital rights through inclusive, data-informed strategies—amplifying voices from the Global South and other underrepresented communities.

An AI Policy Consultancy with a Global Justice Lens

Founded by Harvard Law graduate and former Meta policy lead Chukwuebuka Ogbodo, TAI Advisory bridges the gap between technology, law, and human rights. We specialize in advising on AI governance and digital rights through inclusive, data-informed strategies—amplifying voices from the Global South and other underrepresented communities.
